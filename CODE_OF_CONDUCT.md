# Code of Conduct

VamaoLabs NPC Context is a small open source project. The goal is to keep the work useful, calm, and easy to contribute to.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful when discussing ideas, bugs, tradeoffs, and design choices.
- Assume good intent, but be clear when something is unsafe, misleading, or technically weak.
- Keep feedback focused on the project and the work.
- Help newcomers understand the project without making the process harder than it needs to be.
- Respect maintainer time. Clear reproduction steps and focused pull requests help a lot.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, repeated low-effort comments, or off-topic promotion.
- Using VamaoLabs names or project names to imply official endorsement of unrelated work.

## Reporting

If you see a problem, contact the maintainers privately when possible. For security issues, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other reasonable action to keep the project healthy.
